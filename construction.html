Construction

Park Yasam Mavisehir Complex

About project

Park Yaşam Mavişehir, which is located on 32.104 m² area, consists of 521 residences, 184 offices and shopping malls. The residences consist of 27 different types of apartments in 7 blocks. Consisting of 8871 m² landscaping-green area and 11682 m² covered car park, the project also includes an outdoor, an indoor, a children's swimming pool, 2 tennis courts, 1 basketball court and children's playgrounds.

My contributions

I started working for this project from the very start as civil site engineer. I coordinated the stream improvement, the reinforcement of the soil by jet-grouts, piling and post-grout piling and related subcontractors (36 meters and 21 meters depth). Later on the project I was also responsible from the scheduling and construction of the foundation, superstructures (conventional formwork) being done according to the blueprints and shop drawings. And the construction of sales office and sample flat including finishing works. After the ground reinforcements and rough construction works finished I supervised the finishing construction works.

Fair Izmir

About project

The project is based on a total area of 330.000 m² that is divided as 119.400 m² outdoor and indoor exhibition area and 15.600 m² foyer. It also contains 93,000 m² parking lot for (2,500 vehicles), 16.000 m² area for cafes and restaurants, 4 seminar rooms. There are also 4 halls equipped with a technical infrastructure reinforced with the state of the art technological equipment.

My contributions

At beginning of this project, I was working as civil site engineer responsible for managing and scheduling the foundation and superstructure constructions. But due to some changes in the company structure, I started to work as an estimator for progress payment department. Later on, I was promoted to be the head of progress payments and final accounts. I was in the project from start to soft landing.
My responsibilities at progress payment and final accounts department were estimating, cost-budget and schedule planning, trade-off studies, preparing and estimating the progress payments for both the company and subcontractors.

Mahall Bomonti Izmir

About project

The project is being built on approximately 45.000 m² area, consists of residential, office and commercial areas. Besides 9 historical buildings, that are being restored and contribute to the formation of the social texture of the region, three blocks are being constructed in the project area. It has social facilities such as museums, art houses, cafes and restaurants, open-air concert area.
The project is a candidate with its environmental practices for LEED Platinum Certificate.

My contributions

I involved the project during Design Development Phase of the project. I worked for the owner/general contractor. As Head of Technical Office , I was mainly responsible for cost-budget and schedule planning. Take-off studies and preparation of subcontractors’ contracts.
